Industrial Gases

Greenox Gases provides end-to-end solutions for industrial gas companies—from production to distribution—helping you enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and expand your offerings.

Industrial gases are gases manufactured for use in a wide range of industries such as steelmaking, chemical processing, healthcare, food & beverage, electronics, and energy. They are usually supplied in compressed gas cylinders, bulk liquid tanks, or through on-site generation systems.

1. For Gas Manufacturers

Production & Filling Support

  • Bulk Gas Supply – High-purity O₂, N₂, Ar, CO₂, & other gases in liquid/gaseous form
  • Cylinder Filling Plants – Automated filling systems (5–10,000 cylinders/day)
  • Gas Generators – On-site PSA/VPSA N₂/O₂ plants (10–5,000 Nm³/h)
  • Quality Control – Purity testing (ISO 8573, USP/EP compliance)

Value-Added Services

  • Gas Blending – Custom mixtures (welding, calibration, medical)
  • Bottleneck Analysis – Optimize production efficiency
  • Cylinder Requalification – Hydrostatic testing & recertification

2. For Gas Distributors

Logistics & Inventory Management

  • Bulk Transport Solutions – ISO tanks, tube trailers, microbulk
  • Smart Cylinder Tracking – RFID/GPS-enabled fleet monitoring
  • Automated Vending Machines – 24/7 self-service gas dispensing

Business Growth Support

  • Private Labeling – Custom-branded cylinders & gas blends
  • Demand Forecasting – AI-based inventory optimization
  • Safety Training – Cylinder handling & emergency response

3. For Gas Resellers

Competitive Product Portfolio

  • Specialty Gases – Laser mixes, UHP, rare gases
  • Packaging Options – 2L–50L cylinders, cylinder bundles
  • Bulk Delivery – Microbulk & cryogenic supply

Sales & Marketing Support

  • Technical Datasheets – Ready-to-use product specifications
  • Application Guides – Industry-specific usage recommendations
  • Digital Marketplace – E-commerce integration for B2B sales
