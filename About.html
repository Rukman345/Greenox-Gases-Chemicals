About Us

Greenox Gases is a dynamic, multi-national group that has grown over the past six years into a leading provider of industrial gases and clean energy solutions across the Middle East, Asia, and beyond. Operating through four legal entities and a network of over 10 trusted global partners, we deliver value to clients in more than 20 countries.

Our team combines deep technical expertise with commercial agility to serve diverse industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, energy, defense, and environmental sectors. From supplying critical industrial gases to designing advanced on-site gas systems, we are committed to precision, reliability, and client-focused execution.

Our Products & Services

Comprehensive solutions tailored to optimize operational efficiency, safety, and sustainability across diverse industries.

Gases

  • • Helium
  • • Specialty Gases and Calibration Gases
  • • Bulk gases – Liquid Argon, Liquid N₂O, Ammonia, HCs and Refrigerants
  • • Medical and Industrial Gases – Oxygen, Nitrous Oxide, Argon, and other mixtures
  • • High-purity and Ultra High Purity (UHP) gases

Equipment & Solutions

  • • Cylinders, tanks, and regulators
  • • Telemetry and gas tracking systems
  • • Welding equipment and gas generators
  • • Air Separation Units (ASUs) – small, medium, and large with on-site investment and long-term supply
  • • Plant automation and service solutions

Advanced & Clean Energy

  • • Green hydrogen production
  • • CCUS – Carbon capture, utilization, and storage
  • • Oxygen-enriched combustion for cement and oil & gas
  • • Recovery and safe processing of refrigerants and halons
  • • Sustainable LPG cylinder vending solutions

At Greenox, we are more than suppliers we are strategic partners. Every solution is tailored to optimize operational efficiency, safety, and sustainability. Innovation and environmental responsibility are at the core of everything we do, helping industries transition toward a greener, more efficient future.
