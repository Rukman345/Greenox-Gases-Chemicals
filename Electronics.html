Electronics

Greenox Gases delivers contamination-free gas systems engineered for semiconductor fabrication, display production, and microelectronics assembly.

The electronics industry depends on UHP industrial gases and specialty gases like N₂, O₂, H₂, Ar, He, CO₂, NH₃, and silane-based gases for semiconductor fabrication, displays, solar panels, LEDs, and batteries, making gases essential to modern technology.

Core Semiconductor Gas Products

Bulk Specialties:

  • Hydrogen (99.99995%)
  • Argon (99.9999%)

Etching/Deposition:

  • Fluorine (99.99%)

  • Tungsten Hexafluoride
