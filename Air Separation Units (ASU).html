About

Greenox Gases & Chemicals

Greenox Gases delivers turnkey air separation solutions through strategic partnerships with Teamwin (China) and Yuanhan Energy, combining cutting-edge cryogenic technology with global project execution expertise.

An Air Separation Unit (ASU) is an industrial plant that separates atmospheric air into its primary components — oxygen (O₂), nitrogen (N₂), and argon (Ar) — and sometimes rare gases (neon, krypton, xenon). These units are crucial for producing high-purity gases used in various industries.

ASU Technology Overview

How It Works

Cryogenic air separation liquefies and distills atmospheric air into high-purity gases (O₂, N₂, Ar) at -196°C using:

  • Multi-column distillation (LP/MP/HP columns)
  • Turboexpanders for energy-efficient cooling
  • Molecular sieves for pre-purification (H₂O/CO₂ removal)
