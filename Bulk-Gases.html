Bulk Gases

Greenox Gases delivers a comprehensive range of high purity packaged gases for industrial, medical, and specialty applications. Our solutions are engineered for safety, reliability, and efficiency, with flexible packaging options from cylinders to bulk supply.

Bulk gases are industrial or medical gases supplied in very large volumes, usually delivered in liquid form by cryogenic tankers and stored on-site bulk storage systems. Instead of using multiple cylinders (packaged gases), industries that require continuous, large-scale gas supply rely on bulk installations.

Bulk Argon (Ar)

Ultra-high-purity argon for large-scale shielding and industrial processes.

Purity: 99.999% (5N) for welding, 99.995% (4.5N) for general industrial use
Packaging: ISO tanks (10,000–20,000L)
Pressure/Temp: 5 bar/-186°C (liquid)

Key Industries:

  • • Metal fabrication (TIG/MIG welding)
  • • Semiconductor manufacturing
  • • Aerospace heat treatment

Bulk Helium (He)

Liquid & gaseous helium for critical cooling and leak detection.

Purity: 99.9999% (6N) for MRI, 99.999% (5N) for industrial
Packaging: ISO tanks (10,000L), Cryogenic trailers (20,000–40,000L)
Pressure/Temp: 4K (-269°C) for liquid, 200–300 bar (gaseous)

Key Industries:

  • • Healthcare (MRI superconductors)
  • • Electronics (semiconductor cooling)
  • • Space (rocket pressurization)

Bulk Nitrous Oxide (N₂O)

Medical, food, and industrial-grade N₂O in bulk quantities.

Purity: 99.9% (medical USP), 99.5% (industrial)
Packaging: ISO tanks, Cylinder bundles (500–1,000L)
Pressure: 200–300 bar (gaseous)

Key Industries:

  • • Healthcare (anesthesia systems)
  • • Food (whipped cream production)
  • • Automotive (combustion testing)

Bulk Carbon Dioxide (CO₂)

Food, beverage, and industrial-grade CO₂.

Purity: 99.9% (food-grade), 99.5% (industrial)
Packaging: ISO tanks
Pressure/Temp: 20–57 bar (liquid), -78°C (solid)

Key Industries:

  • • Beverage carbonation
  • • Cold chain logistics
  • • Fire suppression systems

Specialty Bulk Gases

Tailored gas mixtures for niche applications.

Examples: Laser Gases (CO₂/N₂/He mixes), Calibration Gas Blends, Semiconductor Etching Gases (NF₃, SF₆)
Packaging: ISO tanks
Certifications: As per request

Applications:

  • • Laser cutting & engraving

  • • Semiconductor fabrication

Bulk Ammonia (NH₃)

Anhydrous ammonia for refrigeration and chemical synthesis.

Purity: 99.98% (high purity), 99.5% (industrial)
Packaging: ISO tanks (15,000–25,000L), Ton-containers (1,000kg)
Pressure/Temp: 8–12 bar (liquid), -33°C (storage)

Key Industries:

  • • Fertilizer production
  • • Industrial refrigeration
  • • Pharmaceuticals (urea synthesis)
