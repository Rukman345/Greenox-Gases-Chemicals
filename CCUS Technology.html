CCUS technology

Greenox Gases delivers comprehensive Carbon Capture, Utilization & Storage (CCUS) solutions through strategic partnerships with Teamwin (modular capture systems) and Yuanhan Energy (AI-driven process optimization), enabling industries to achieve net-zero emissions while monetizing CO₂.

CCUS Technology encompasses the complete value chain: capturing CO₂ from industrial sources (post-combustion, pre-combustion, or direct air capture), purifying and compressing it to specification, and either utilizing it in industrial processes or storing it permanently in geological formations.

1. Carbon Capture Systems

a) Post-Combustion (Flue Gas)

Solvent-Based (MEA/DEEA)

  • Capacity: 10-5,000 TPD CO₂
  • Purity: ≥ 99.5% CO₂
  • Yuanhan's low-energy solvent regeneration
  • Teamwin's corrosion-resistant absorber towers (2205 duplex SS)

b) Pre-Combustion (Syngas)

Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA)

  • CO₂ recovery rate: 85-95%
  • Integration with SMR/gasification
  • Yuanhan's low-energy solvent regeneration
  • Teamwin's corrosion-resistant absorber towers (2205 duplex SS)

c) Oxy-Fuel Combustion

Partnership with Yuanhan's OECS systems for high-concentration CO₂ streams
